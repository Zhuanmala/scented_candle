- "Authorize sending supplier messages to these 10 Alibaba suppliers."
- "Authorize sample spending up to EUR 220."
- "Authorize posting the 14-day content calendar to Instagram after I approve each post."
- "Authorize creating a waitlist form with this Google account."

Without explicit authorization, I will prepare assets and analysis but stop before external actions.
