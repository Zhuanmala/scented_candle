# Waitlist / Tester Form Questions

Recommended tool: Google Forms, Tally, Typeform, Notion Form, or Shopify waitlist app.

## Form Title

Scented Candle Blind Test / Duftkerzen Blindtest

## Intro

We are developing a small premium scented candle collection inspired by theatre, textiles, and interior spaces. We are currently looking for testers in Germany and early followers who want launch updates.

## Questions

1. Name.
2. Email.
3. City.
4. Are you currently in Germany? yes/no.
5. Which language do you prefer? English/German.
6. Do you usually buy scented candles? never/sometimes/often.
7. Would you join a 15-minute blind scent test? yes/no/maybe.
8. Would you consider buying a premium scented candle at EUR 29-39 if the scent and packaging feel right? yes/no/maybe.
9. Which scent direction sounds most interesting?
   - Winter House: fir, cedarwood, dry orange peel, beeswax, subtle spice.
   - Room After Rain: white tea, linen, light wood, clean air.
   - Backstage Wool: old wood, paper, wool, curtain, soft smoke.

11. Optional Instagram/TikTok handle.
