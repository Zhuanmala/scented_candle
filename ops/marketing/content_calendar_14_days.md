# 14-Day Content Calendar

Purpose: build audience and recruit testers without pretending the product is already launched.

## Content Rules

- Say "in development" or "testing".
- Do not claim therapeutic effects.
- Do not claim natural, sustainable, non-toxic, or EU-compliant until verified.
- Ask for participation and feedback, not just likes.
- Track action: DMs, comments, email signups, tester applications.
