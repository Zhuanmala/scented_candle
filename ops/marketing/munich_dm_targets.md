Purpose: recruit first testers without spam, paid ads, or misleading sales claims.

- Munich city and public event pages have visible English-language resources.

- Munich expats

## Outreach Rules

- Send no more than 20 manual DMs per day per platform in this sprint.
- Personalize the first line when possible.
- Do not ask for purchase, deposit, or payment.
- Do not imply samples are ready if they are not.
- Stop after one no-response follow-up.
- Record strong replies manually in `ops/testing/blind_test_results.csv` later only after tester consent.

## DM Opening

Hi [Name], I am developing a small candle concept in Munich inspired by theatre, textiles, and interior spaces.

It is not launched yet. I am looking for people in or near Munich for a blind scent test and honest feedback.

Would you be open to joining the tester waitlist? It should take around 15 minutes once samples are ready.
