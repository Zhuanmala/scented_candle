# Dried Flower Sample Campaign

Source: GitHub Issue #6  
Purpose: test whether the dried-flower candle visual and two scent directions can generate comments, DMs, and waitlist actions on Instagram and TikTok.

## Positioning

This is a sample preference campaign, not a product listing campaign.

Logistics assumption for physical Munich preference tests:

- User-provided Shanghai to Munich student-carry benchmark: EUR 15/kg.
- Approximate carry cost: 100g = EUR 1.50, 200g = EUR 3.00, 500g = EUR 7.50, 1kg = EUR 15.00.
- This makes a few physical samples cost-effective for scent/packaging feedback.
- This does not replace compliant commercial import, product documentation, or the Alibaba EU-document supplier path.

Public content should ask people to choose between two sample directions:

- Roman Chamomile: quieter, soft, bedroom/evening mood.
- Golden Coast: warmer, floral, sunlit gift mood.

The content can mention the sample format shown by the current batch:

- dried flowers
- soy wax
- essential-oil style scent
- natural-material visual
- sleep/evening mood, phrased as atmosphere rather than a health promise

Do not say:

- direct purchase calls to action
- claims that the samples are ready for German market distribution
- claims that the samples have completed European compliance review
- medical or health-effect claims
- guaranteed ingredient claims unless confirmed by supplier documents

Preferred wording:

- "sleepy evening mood"
- "bedside atmosphere"
- "calm room scent"
- "based on a current sample batch"
- "which direction should we test in Munich?"

Avoid wording:

- direct health-effect promises
- disease or insomnia language
- certification language
- finished-market-readiness language

## Success Metrics

Track for the first 48 hours after posting:

- comments with CHAMOMILE
- comments with COAST
- DMs with TEST
- waitlist submissions
- people asking price
- people asking scent notes
- people saying they are in or near Munich

Decision after 48 hours:

- If one scent direction gets at least 2x the other, use it as the first physical-test direction.
- If packaging gets weak feedback, keep scent learning but do not use this packaging route for the EU-compliant supplier path.
- If there are no comments or DMs, improve the visual format before posting again.
