## Three Scent Directions

### Scene I: Winter House

Fir, cedarwood, dry orange peel, beeswax, subtle spice. A winter room with warm light, not a sweet Christmas cliché.

### Scene II: Room After Rain

White tea, linen, light wood, soft soap, clean air. A calm home scent for people who dislike heavy perfumes.

### Scene III: Backstage Wool

Old wood, paper, wool, curtain, soft smoke. A more atmospheric scent inspired by theatre spaces and material memory.
