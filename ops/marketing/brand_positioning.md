# Working Brand Positioning

Status: working draft. Do not register or print before user approval.

## Strategic Position

A small atmospheric candle studio inspired by theatre, textile, light, and interior spaces.

We are not selling "just a scent." We are testing whether a candle can feel like a small scene: light, material, and atmosphere in one object.

## German Direction

Duft als Sprache des Raumes.  
Kleine Szenen aus Licht, Material und Atmosphäre.

## Chinese Direction

气味不是装饰，而是一种空间语言。  
每一支蜡烛都是一幕可以被点燃的小型场景。

## What To Avoid

- Cheap candle brand.
- Generic soy wax candle.
- Healing, medical, sleep, or anxiety claims.
- Heavy Chinese souvenir style.
- Overly sweet Christmas novelty.
- Trend-only Instagram candle.

## Visual Direction

- Warm neutral background.
- Matte paper.
- Off-white, warm grey, charcoal, tobacco brown.
- Minimal typography.
- Theatre cues through naming and story, not decorative props.
- No plastic-heavy presentation.

## Working Fragrance Names

These are placeholders for validation, not final trademarks.

### Scene I: Winter House

Direction: fir, cedarwood, dry orange peel, beeswax, subtle spice, warm cabin light.

Use case: winter gift, Christmas market, home atmosphere.

### Scene II: Room After Rain

Direction: white tea, linen, light wood, soft soap, clean air after rain.

Use case: safe year-round home scent, lower entry barrier.

### Scene III: Backstage Wool

Direction: old wood, paper, wool, curtain, soft smoke, fading bouquet.

Use case: brand signature and theatre differentiation.

## Short Launch Copy

We are developing a small scented candle collection for homes that care about atmosphere. Inspired by theatre, textiles, and interior spaces, each candle is designed as a small scene rather than a single note.

We are currently testing the first three scent directions with local users in Germany. If you want to join the blind scent test or be notified when the first small batch is ready, leave your email.
