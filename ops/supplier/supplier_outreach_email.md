# Supplier Outreach Template

Use this for Alibaba International suppliers. Do not send through 1688 unless translated and adapted; 1688 suppliers may not understand EU documentation requirements.

## Initial Message

Hello,

We are developing a small premium scented candle collection for the German / EU market. We are looking for existing samples first, and potentially a small private-label test batch after validation.

Product direction:
- 180g-220g scented candle
- Soy wax or rapeseed wax blend preferred
- Glass or simple ceramic container
- Cotton wick preferred
- Minimal premium packaging
- Three fragrance directions: winter/gift, clean home, theatre/signature
- First validation batch after sample approval may be 50-150 pcs, then 150-300 pcs if test results are strong

Before custom development, we would like to buy existing samples.

Important questions:
1. Do you have experience exporting scented candles to the EU, especially Germany?
2. Can you provide SDS for each fragrance/candle?
3. Can you provide IFRA certificate for each fragrance?
4. Can you provide allergen declaration?
5. Can you provide REACH compliance statement?
6. Can you support CLP label information for each fragrance?
7. Do you have EN 15493, EN 15494, or EN 15426 test reports or previous EU candle safety documentation?
8. What is your MOQ for private label, custom scent, custom label, and custom box?
9. What existing samples can you send now?
10. Do you support Alibaba Trade Assurance orders?

Please send:
- Product catalogue
- Existing sample options
- Wax options
- Fragrance list
- Packaging options
- MOQ and sample cost
- Estimated sample shipping cost to Germany
- Example EU compliance documents
- Estimated production lead time

Thank you.

## Follow-Up If They Say "No Problem" But Send No Documents

Thank you. Before we purchase samples or discuss a test batch, we need to review example documents.

Could you please send sample or redacted examples of:
- SDS
- IFRA certificate
- allergen declaration
- REACH compliance statement
- CLP label support/example
- EN 15493 / EN 15494 / EN 15426 related test reports or candle safety documents, if available

We do not need confidential fragrance formulas. Redacted examples are acceptable for the first screening.

## Follow-Up For Existing Samples

For existing samples, please recommend 2-3 options closest to these directions:

1. Winter/Gift: fir, cedarwood, dry orange peel, beeswax, subtle spice, warm wood cabin.
2. Clean Home: white tea, linen, soft soap, light wood, clean air, room after rain.
3. Theatre/Signature: old wood, paper, wool, curtain, soft smoke, backstage atmosphere.

Please include:
- Wax type
- Wax weight
- Burn time
- Fragrance load if available
- Wick type
- Container material
- Sample price
- Shipping price to Germany

## Rejection Template

Thank you for your response. For this project we need stronger EU/Germany documentation and low-risk sample testing before moving forward, so we will not continue at this stage.

We may reconnect later if our requirements change.
