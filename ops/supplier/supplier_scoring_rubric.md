# Supplier Scoring Rubric

Score each supplier from 1-5 in every dimension. Reject suppliers that fail compliance or safety gates even if pricing is attractive.

## Weighted Score

| Dimension | Weight | What Good Looks Like |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| EU/Germany export experience | 15% | Can describe prior EU customers and documentation, not just say "yes" |
| Compliance documents | 25% | Provides SDS, IFRA, allergen declaration, REACH statement, CLP support, and candle safety documents |
| Sample quality | 20% | Premium scent, stable burn, clean wax surface, good container and packaging |
| Burn safety | 15% | No serious soot, tunneling, overheating, cracking, or unstable flame |
| Small-batch flexibility | 10% | Will send samples and support 50-150 or 150-300 pc test batch |
| Communication | 10% | Specific, timely, clear, willing to document specs |
| Price reasonableness | 5% | Competitive but not suspiciously low |

## Hard Reject Gates

Reject if any are true:
- Refuses to provide SDS or IFRA examples.
- Does not understand CLP label requirements.
- Cannot explain EU/Germany export experience.
- Refuses Trade Assurance or written order specifications.
- Provides only polished images and no real sample pathway.
- Quote is unusually low and document quality is weak.
- Communication is vague after two follow-ups.

## Decision Labels

- `A - sample now`: Buy existing sample.
- `B - keep warm`: Good but not first sample priority.
- `C - needs documents`: Continue only if documents arrive.
- `D - reject`: Do not spend time or money.

## First Target

Contact 10-15 suppliers.

Expected funnel:
- 5 suppliers with credible responses.
- 3 suppliers shortlisted.
- 2 suppliers sampled.
- 1 main supplier plus 1 backup after testing.
