## Contact Order

1. Verified supplier / Trade Assurance suppliers first.
2. Suppliers showing EU export or compliance language second.
3. Suppliers with low MOQ and credible samples third.
4. Raw wax suppliers only if finished candle suppliers cannot support rapeseed blend.

## First Pass Evaluation

Advance to shortlist only if:
- Gives MOQ and sample cost clearly.
- Confirms existing samples are available.
- Provides or agrees to provide redacted EU document examples.
- Does not dodge SDS/IFRA/CLP questions.
- Supports written order specs and Trade Assurance.

## Sample Buying Decision

Buy samples from at most 2-3 suppliers in the EUR 220 sample budget.

Priority sample mix:
- 1 Winter/Gift direction.
- 1 Clean Home direction.
- 1 Theatre/Signature or closest atmospheric woody/paper/leather/wool direction.

Do not pay for full custom formulation in this stage.
