# Compliance Document Request Checklist

This is a business screening checklist, not legal advice.

## Supplier Must Provide Before Production

For each final fragrance/candle:

- SDS.
- IFRA certificate.
- Allergen declaration.
- REACH compliance statement.
- CLP label support or proposed CLP label text.
- Fragrance documentation without necessarily revealing confidential formula.
- Packaging material information.
- Batch number system.
- Product photos before shipment.
- Commercial invoice.
- Packing list.

## Candle Safety Documents To Request

- EN 15493 fire safety test report or related documentation.
- EN 15494 product safety label documentation or label approach.
- EN 15426 sooting behavior test report or related documentation.
- Any EN 16738 / combustible air freshener emissions documentation if available.

## Label Information To Prepare

Front label:
- Brand name.
- Fragrance name.
- Duftkerze / Scented Candle.
- Net weight.
- Short scent description.

Back / bottom label:
- Safety warnings.
- Burn instructions.
- Ingredient/allergen statements as required by CLP/SDS.
- Manufacturer.
- Importer / responsible economic operator details for EU/Germany.
- Batch number.
- Country of origin.
- Pictograms where applicable.

## Claims To Avoid Unless Evidence Exists

- 100% natural.
- non-toxic.
- chemical-free.
- therapeutic.
- sleep improvement.
- anxiety relief.
- eco-friendly.
- zero emission.
- sustainable.
- hypoallergenic.

## Safer Claim Direction

- Made with soy wax blend.
- Recyclable glass container, if verified.
- FSC-certified paper packaging, only if the specific packaging is certified.
- Designed for a calm home atmosphere.
- Inspired by theatre, textile, and interior spaces.
- Carefully developed in small batches.
