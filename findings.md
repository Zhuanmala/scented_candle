## Compliance Findings

- `[KNOWN]` Relevant compliance areas include GPSR, REACH, CLP, SDS, IFRA, allergen declaration, and candle safety standards EN 15493, EN 15494, EN 15426.
- `[KNOWN]` IFRA certificates help document fragrance standard conformity for intended use, but do not replace a full safety assessment.
- `[INFERRED, HIGH]` Supplier claims are not enough. For any formal sale in Germany/EU, product-level documentation and importer/responsible operator details must be handled carefully.
