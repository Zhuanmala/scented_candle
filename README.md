# Scented Candle Commercial Validation

This repository manages a EUR 500 commercial validation project for a premium scented candle concept targeting the German/EU market.

The project is not treated as a full product launch yet. The current goal is to validate scent preference, packaging perception, price acceptance, supplier quality, and compliance-document feasibility before committing to production.

## Current Position

- Market: Germany / EU.
- Product concept: premium scented candle inspired by theatre, textile, light, and interior spaces.
- Budget: EUR 500 for validation.
- Launch posture: validation first, no production order until Go criteria are met.
- Current phase: project assets prepared; external execution waits on account access and explicit authorization.

## Core Rule

Do not spend money, place orders, publish content, send outbound supplier messages, collect deposits, or log into user accounts without explicit authorization.

## Go Criteria

Move to a 50-150 unit micro-batch only if:

- At least 30 valid blind-test responses are collected.
- At least 60% of testers consider the product giftable.
- At least 30% are willing to pay EUR 29 or more.
- At least 10 waitlist/preorder signals are collected.
- At least 3 strong buying signals come from non-friends.
- Supplier documents are credible enough for EU/Germany review.
- Sample burn tests show no obvious safety issues.
